Instructions

  • To toggle between road construction and car instatiation click the spacebar
  • When in road construction mode
    • Left click to create a single road tile
    • Drag the mouse while pressing down the right click to continuously build road tiles
    • Right click to remove an end road tile
    • Right click a car to remove it
  • When in car instantiation mode
    • Left click to create a single car sprite
    • Right click a car to remove it
